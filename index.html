The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in apperance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare,known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinction, the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantice around the world. The beagle is a popular pet due to its size,good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.
